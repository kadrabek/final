Must see Sites in Arizona!

There are many sites that Arizona has to offer. I want to show you four sites that are a must see for history buffs or for a family adventure.

The Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is a good way to start your vacation here in Arizona. It is a must see site while you are here!
It has a great history that goes back to almost 10,000 years ago! The canyon has been home to many people over the years including 11 contemporary tribes that have cultural ties to the canyon itself.
The Grand Canyon National Park is continuing to preserve and protect the human history of the Grand Canyon.
This is definitly a must see as there is so much to explore from the history to the sites that it has to offer.

Antelope Canyon

Antelope Canyon is a unique place to visit here in Arizona and will not disappoint you in the slightest.
The canyon itself is beautiful and is known for it's photography of the canyon. You can hike in the canyon along with tour guides helping along the way.
There are plenty of hotels nearby the canyon if you plan on staying out there.
Going here is a must for the sites the canyon has to offer.

Tombstone

Have you ever wanted to go back in time to the wild west? Well Tombstone has you covered!
Tombstone is a town full of history and even spirits! The history of Tombstone make it worth checking out.
Especially if you like old western movies. You'll be teleported straight into one when you step foot into Tombstone.

Montezuma Castle National Monument

This National Monument is dedicated to preserving Native American Culture. It is unique as the monument itself is nestled high into the cliff of a mountain.
The Sinagua were residents who lived in dwellings at the monument around 1050 CE. Going to this monument will give you more information about Sinagua culture
and about the monument itself. Learning more about the history of the Sinagua is what makes this monument a must see site!
