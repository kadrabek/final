Sunsets and Sunrises.

Sunsets and Sunrises can be seen everywhere but Arizona might hold the best of these two in the sky.
We take pride in our sunsets and sunrises here in Arizona. The beauty that paints the sky in the early morning or the beginnings of night are magnificent.
Here is some more information on both of these topics that litter our skies in Arizona that might leave you in awe.

Sunrises

Sunrises are a way to tell that the day is starting. It can be seen all around the world and is the first thing in the sky that we can observe.
Arizona has some beautiful sunrises that await the residents everyday. There are many ways to see these sunrises in the best spots.
Some of the best spots to see them are:

1. Camelback Mountain
2. Lost Dutchman State Park
3. East Wetlands Restoration Area
4. Saguaro National Park (East District)

There are plenty more of these sites that you can personally look up or explore in Arizona.

Sunsets

Sunsets can also be seen all over the world. It is the Earth's way of saying that the day is over.
Sunsets in Arizona have beautiful colors to them that make them stand out. Seeing them in person will make you appreciate the world around you.
Here are some good spots to see the sunsets of Arizona:

1. Lost Dutchman State Park
2. Picacho Peak State Park
3. Catalina State Park
4. Slide Rock State Park
5. Red Rock State Park

Plus so many more spots. If you have the chance to visit any of these places around the time of sunsets appear then observe these beautiful colors in the sky.
